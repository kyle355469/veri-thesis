## Unresolved Assumptions
- The SD card interface timing (setup/hold) is compatible with the chosen sd_clk frequency.
- The SD_BUS_W is fixed to 4 bits; no support for 1‑bit mode is implemented.
- The Wishbone master burst type (CTI/BTE) is always 0 (single transfer).
- The asynchronous reset of sd_clock_divider is active‑high and does not require a synchronous de‑assertion.
- The BD_SIZE is a power of two (<= 256) to simplify pointer wrap‑around logic.
- The external SD card model used in simulation correctly implements the SD protocol timing and CRC checks.
